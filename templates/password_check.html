How does it work?

We use the Have I Been Pwned API with k-anonymity. Your password is hashed locally, and only the first 5 characters of the hash are sent. This means your actual password never leaves your device.

Is it safe?

Absolutely! The k-anonymity model ensures your password cannot be reconstructed or identified. Even if someone intercepted the request, they couldn't determine your password.

What if it's compromised?

If your password is found in a breach, change it immediately on all accounts where you use it. Use a unique password for each service and consider using a password manager.
